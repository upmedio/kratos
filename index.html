Terms and Conditions:

Should our hard cap of USD 32 million be reached before the stated period, we will end the sale in advance and in the event of failure to reach our soft cap of USD 3 million, we will return the monies raised over the period of the campaign back to the contributors.

If and when after a period of one year from the issuing date, the value of the BIG tokens falls below 15% of the issue price, BIG Token Limited is committed to buy back BIG tokens from the market at the price of 15% above the issue price if the holders of the BIG tokens choose to sell a portion or all of their BIG tokens to BIG Token Limited by using funds from the net interest received from the loans to the land sponsors and the principal repayments of the loans.

The land sponsors will receive up to 80% of the loaned amount in fiat currencies. Another 10% of the loaned amount will be borrowed in an equivalent amount of BIG tokens to be held in trust by BIG Grp Co., Limited until the load is fully paid. This acts as a safeguard should the land sponsor defaults on interest payments, the sponsors can use this 10% of the loan amount in tokens as interest payments.

The land sponsors will also jointly set aside up to 10% of their loan amount to a common pool to be held in trust by BIG Token Limited to be used to maintain price stability and reduce volatility of the token.

Each BIG Token will be offered at 0.0002 ETH with a minimum purchase of 10,000 Big Tokens. Should the campaign be under subscribed, the remaining tokens that were offered for the public ICO will be destroyed, so as to avoid dilution of the token value.
